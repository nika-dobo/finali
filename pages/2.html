Super Mario World is a classic platformer game for the Super Nintendo Entertainment System (SNES) where you control Mario or Luigi and rescue Princess Toadstool from Bowser. You can play it online for free on Arcade Spot, along with other SNES games and Mario games. Enjoy the side-scrolling graphics, power-ups, and boss fights in this fun game. Super Mario World is also the sixth entry in the Super Mario series and a direct sequel to Super Mario Bros. 3. It introduces a large variety of new gameplay mechanics, such as an expanded and less linear world map and the ability to save the game. You can also ride on Yoshi, a friendly dinosaur who can eat enemies and lay eggs that can be used as platforms or projectiles.

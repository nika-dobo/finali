pasmafobia

It is a 4 player online co-op psychological horror game developed and published by British indie game studio Kinetic Games. The game is based primarily on the popular hobby of ghost hunting. You and your team of paranormal investigators will enter haunted locations filled with paranormal activity and try to gather as much evidence as you can. The game became available in early access through Steam for Microsoft Windows with virtual reality support in September 2020.

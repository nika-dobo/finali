cs:go

Counter-Strike: Global Offensive (CS:GO) is a popular tactical shooter game that expands upon the team-based first-person shooter gameplay the original Counter-Strike pioneered when it launched in 1999. It features new maps, characters, and weapons and delivers updated versions of the classic Counter-Strike maps like Dust, Inferno, Nuke, Train, and more. In addition, CS:GO introduces new game modes like Arms Race, Flying Scoutsman, and Wingman, and features online matchmaking and Competitive Skill Groups. The game is available on Steam and is free to play.
